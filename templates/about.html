Our Insider Threat Detection System uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze employee behavior patterns and identify potential security risks within organizations.

Analyzed Features

Activity Patterns

Digital activity and behavioral patterns that indicate normal vs. suspicious behavior.

  • Login frequency and patterns
  • HTTP request behaviors
  • Email communication patterns
  • After-hours activity levels
  • Weekend usage patterns
Device Usage

USB device interactions and external storage usage patterns.

  • USB connection frequency
  • Device disconnect patterns
  • Unusual timing indicators
  • Anomalous usage detection
Psychological Factors

Big Five personality traits and psychological risk indicators.

  • Openness: Creativity and openness to experience
  • Conscientiousness: Organization and responsibility
  • Extraversion: Social engagement level
  • Agreeableness: Cooperation and trust
  • Neuroticism: Emotional stability
Behavioral Metrics

Advanced metrics and ratios that reveal behavioral anomalies.

  • Activity ratios and correlations
  • Entropy and variability measures
  • Risk assessment scores
  • Baseline deviation indicators
